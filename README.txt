Week5 Project - London Crime between 2012-2016

Datasets:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jboysen/london-crime
https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/uk-house-price-index
https://www.openintro.org/data/index.php?data=london_boroughs

The aim of the project is to answer the following questions:

1) Which regions have the highest/ lowest number of crimes? 
2) What are the most commonly committed crimes/ least? 
3) Does seasonality affect no. of crime?
4) Is the no. of crime decreasing/ increasing?
5) Is the no. of crime correlated to average house price of a region? - Is burglary higher in areas with higher priced houses?
